Frequently Asked Questions

How do I transfer my domain to Alpha1TecLabs?
Transferring your domain is easy. First, unlock your domain at your current registrar and get the authorization code. Then, initiate the transfer from your Alpha1TecLabs dashboard and enter the code. The transfer usually completes within 5-7 days.
What's included with domain registration?
All domain registrations include free WHOIS privacy protection, DNS management, domain forwarding, and 24/7 support. You also get free email forwarding with up to 100 aliases.
Can I register a domain without hosting?
Yes! You can register a domain without purchasing hosting. You can use our DNS management tools to point your domain anywhere, or add hosting later when you're ready.
How long does domain registration take?
Most domain registrations are instant. Once you complete your purchase, your domain will be active and ready to use within minutes.
